# Impact of Poor Digital Communication

A real-world example of poor digital communication can be seen when students post inappropriate content on social media, believing it to be private. In many cases, such posts have been shared publicly, leading to disciplinary actions or even loss of academic or career opportunities. For instance, offensive messages in a private group chat can easily be leaked and viewed by authorities.

This situation highlights that digital communication is rarely truly private. To avoid such issues, students should follow the “think before you post” rule and maintain professionalism across all platforms. Being respectful and responsible online helps protect one’s reputation and future opportunities.
