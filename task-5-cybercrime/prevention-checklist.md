# 🛡️ Stay Safe Online – Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

- Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money.
- Do not approve unknown Collect/Request notifications.
- Always verify QR codes before scanning.
- Avoid sharing OTP, bank details, or UPI screenshots.
- Enable biometric lock on payment apps.
- Check receiver name before making payments.
- Avoid clicking suspicious links from WhatsApp or SMS.
- Do not use public Wi-Fi for banking transactions.
- Enable SMS/email transaction alerts.
- Install apps only from official Play Store/App Store.

# 💰 UPI / Financial Safety
- Never accept help from strangers for refunds or cashback.
- If someone asks for PIN to receive money, it is a scam.
